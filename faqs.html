FAQs

Who are B2T?

B2T is a leading crypto-native liquidity provider for institutions globally.

What do you offer as a liquidity provider?

B2T provides deep, reliable liquidity and a full suite of OTC products, including spot, derivatives, funding, and structured loans, tailored to institutional needs.

Can anyone trade with B2T?

B2T provides services exclusively to institutional clients, not retail investors or consumers.

How do I contact B2T?

Use the contact form or email us at info@b2t.com.

What is the overnight funding rate? How is this calculated?

The overnight funding rate is variable, typically dependent on spot and derivatives market conditions. Contact us for more details.

Do you have minimum/maximum trade sizes?

B2T can facilitate trades of all sizes, from small to multimillion dollar blocks, depending on the product and client profile.

What is your Equal Employment Opportunities policy?

B2T is committed to providing a fair, inclusive, and diverse workplace. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, gender, age, religion, or any other protected characteristic.

What are OTC liquidity providers?

OTC liquidity providers offer deep, reliable liquidity for institutional clients, enabling seamless execution and settlement of large trades outside of public exchanges.

How are you different from other providers?

B2T combines proprietary technology, deep market expertise, and a client-first approach to deliver best-in-class service and execution for institutions globally.

What are Contracts for Difference (CFDs)?

CFDs are derivative products that allow clients to gain exposure to price movements in crypto assets without owning the underlying asset.

What are the margin requirements for trading CFDs?

Margin requirements vary by product and client profile. Contact us for more details.
