Social Impact and CSR

B2T is committed to supporting social mobility and corporate social responsibility initiatives globally.

B2T is proud to harness our talent and drive for social impact, in conjunction with valuable non-profit and educational partners. We are committed to driving sustainable mobility by creating meaningful opportunities for individuals and communities, and by supporting charitable causes and social responsibility initiatives worldwide.

Social Impact

We believe that education is the most powerful lever for social mobility. Through partnerships with local schools, charities, and non-profit organizations, B2T is dedicated to hosting impactful student career development programs and supporting social mobility initiatives globally.

By focusing our time, resources, and values, we aim to support individuals in overcoming barriers and achieving success for themselves and their communities.

Future Frontiers
B2T joins forces with Future Frontiers to support social mobility by hosting student career development programs and other impactful initiatives.
Corporate Social Responsibility

In the CSR space, each employee receives a day per year to focus on an initiative close to their heart. B2T supports a range of charitable causes and encourages employees to volunteer and give back to their communities.

Our London office has developed a relationship with the FareShare food redistribution network, helping to provide meals for those in need. We also support a range of other local and international charities through fundraising and volunteering.
