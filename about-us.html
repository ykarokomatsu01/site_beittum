About us

24/7_
Global crypto liquidity

B2T is a leading crypto-native liquidity provider across market conditions for institutions globally.

B2T is a digital asset pioneer building the ecosystem of the future.

The firm has unlocked institutional access to crypto by providing reliable liquidity across market conditions. B2T’s success is built on crypto-native technology and continuous product innovation, making it the partner of choice for diverse institutions globally.

Founded in 2015, B2T remains a standalone company. Headquartered in the UK, with offices in the US and Japan.

Major milestones

While many firms say they’re bridging the gap between traditional and crypto markets, B2T truly is.

  • 2022: Launched Options Chain, the first electronic OTC options trading product. Became ISDA and FIA members. Launched structured loans product, DDAIs. Launched B2T CORE OTC Trading Platform.
  • 2021: Executed a crypto non-deliverable forward (NDF). Launched our options desk and funding desk.
  • 2020: Launched world’s first bitcoin/gold derivative. Japanese financial group SBI acquires cryptocurrency trading firm B2T.
  • 2019: First to offer an executable streaming price feed. Opening of the US branch. First EU cryptocurrency firm to receive a regulatory licence to offer derivatives as a principal.
  • 2018: First OTC streaming price feed in the market. Opening of the Tokyo branch.
  • 2017: B2T branding launch.
  • 2016: First single dealer platform in the industry.
